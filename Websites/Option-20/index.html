RECRUITMENT / PLACEMENT SERVICES

We comprehend the significance of having an ideal choice for an occupation. We trust that each position, regardless of it being low or high up in the hierarchy, is similarly critical for the association and consequently we put in our earnest attempts to enable you to enlist the best of work force.

Our expertise in understanding the job demands and skill set requirements necessary to be successful in the job, help us shortlist the best talent for our clients. We ensure the best talent is made available to our clients within the given time span.

EXECUTIVE SEARCH

We understand that every executive search assignment brings unique challenges. Executive hiring is one of the most crucial and important part of recruitment as it generally involves hiring talent at leadership positions.. Human resource is the pivotal element in success of any business, thus our focus is on finding right candidates in terms of experience, skill sets who can contribute to our client’s success. HirePoint offers recruitment services that help clients reduce cost and time associated with hiring the right talent. These positions generally shape the future of a company and hence adequate care and diligence is necessary when recruiting for them.

FLEXI STAFFING

Flexi Staffing refers to manpower resources being hired on a Short or Long term basis. HirePoint offers a prompt & reliable temporary staffing solution that offers the ability to build staff strength without absorbing them full time and keep projects moving.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS OUTSOURCING

The entire recruitment process is outsourced to us, wherein we take care of every aspect of recruitment, while the client can focus on their core business activities.

We effectively handle the entire recruitment process of our clients with speed of delivery, quality of talent, responsibility & accountability. We provide the following customized range of services always keeping in mind the client’s needs.
